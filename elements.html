My Background

My name is Carla, and technology has always fascinated me --- it's like a puzzle waiting to be solved and I'm pretty good at puzzles.
I began my journey exploring the realms of videogames development, and that's where I discovered my love for programming.
The initial struggle turned at some point into a small pay for the satisfaction of watching a program that I built come to life.
It made every struggle worthwhile.
My story took a small turn when I ventured later into computer science aiming to get deeper knowledge on programming, and python quickly became my programming language of choice--the sleekness of the snake symbolizing the elegance of its syntax.
Yet, that wasn't the end of the story (I'm almost done... for now), a casual YouTube stroll introduced me to the world of data analytics, opening up an exhilarating new chapter in my journey.
And here we are.
Amid this technological odyssey, I was refining my soft-skills while working in various people-related roles. These experiences offered me opportunities to develop my capabilities and collaborate with diverse teams which I will always appreciate.
